About Pulse

Welcome to Pulse Multispecialty Hospital, one of the most trusted and best-rated hospitals in Tathawade, Pimpri-Chinchwad (PCMC). Since 2014, we have been delivering comprehensive healthcare services, including 24x7 emergency care, ICU services, OPD and IPD treatments, and specialized surgeries. Backed by a team of experienced doctors, certified ICU specialists, and healthcare professionals, we combine medical expertise with advanced diagnostic and surgical technologies.

Our hospital is known for ethical practices, compassionate care, and positive patient outcomes. Whether you need emergency care in Wakad, critical care in Hinjewadi, or multispecialty consultation in PCMC, Pulse is committed to your well-being with integrity and precision. We follow strict clinical protocols to ensure safe, effective, and transparent treatment at every step of your health journey.
