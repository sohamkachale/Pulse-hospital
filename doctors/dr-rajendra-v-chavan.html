Dr. Rajendra Vishwambhar Chavan

Dr. Rajendra V. Chavan is a senior Consultant & Interventional Cardiologist with more than 10 years experience.His qualifications include MBBS(JJ Hospital, Mumbai), MD (Medicine), DM Cardiology (Nair Hospital, Mumbai).He has also worked as an assistant professor in Cardiology at Nair Hospital, Mumbai for 6 years.He is an expert in management of Hypertension, Coronary Artery Disease & Heart Failure.He has extensive experience in Interventional cardiology having performed more than 10000 procedures like Coronary Angiography, Coronary Angioplasty, Pacemakers, Balloon Valvuloplasty etc.He is attached with major multispeciality hospitals in PCMC.He is available at the clinic for cardiac consultation, 2D echo, Stress test (TMT), Holter, ABPM.

  • Chest pain evaluation, breathlessness work-up and cardiac risk assessment.
  • Echocardiography, TMT/Stress testing, Holter & ambulatory BP monitoring.
  • Interventional care: angiography, angioplasty & stent management (as indicated).
  • Heart failure, hypertension, cholesterol & diabetes-related heart disease care.

Professional Skills

Non-invasive and interventional cardiology, preventive cardiology, complex case evaluation and evidence-based optimization of cardiac medications with structured follow-up.

Cardiology Specialist

Comprehensive management of coronary artery disease, rhythm issues, heart failure and lifestyle-related cardiac risks—delivered with clarity, safety and compassionate care.

  • DM – Cardiology (2013) – Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik.
  • MD – General Medicine (2008) – Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Marathwada University.
  • MBBS (2003) – Maharashtra University of Health Sciences, Nashik.

Conditions & Care

Visit for accurate diagnosis, clear explanations and step-wise treatment plans tailored to your heart health goals.

  • Coronary artery disease, angina, post-angioplasty/stent follow-up.
  • Arrhythmias, palpitations, syncope evaluation and medical management.
  • Heart failure optimization, device therapy guidance (as indicated).
  • Preventive cardiology: lipid, BP, diabetes & lifestyle risk control.
