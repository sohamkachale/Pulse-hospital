Dr. Tushar Kharade

Dr. Tushar Kharade is a trusted General Physician (Internal Medicine) in Tathawade, PCMC. He provides evidence-based diagnosis and treatment for acute illnesses like fever and infections, and long-term management for diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders and cholesterol problems. His patient-first approach focuses on clear counselling, rational medicines and preventive health checks to keep you healthy and active.

  • Fever, viral illnesses, throat/chest infections and gastrointestinal complaints.
  • Diabetes care: evaluation, medication titration, diet & lifestyle planning.
  • Hypertension, lipid and thyroid disorder management with regular follow-ups.
  • Preventive health checkups, cardiac risk screening and second opinions.

Professional Skills

Internal Medicine practice with focus on accurate diagnosis, safe prescriptions, lifestyle modification, vaccination advice and continuity of care for multi-system conditions.

Clinical Interests

Comprehensive primary care for adults — acute infections, metabolic disorders, cardiovascular risk reduction and long-term disease prevention through structured follow-up plans.

  • Diabetes & Hypertension – Integrated medication and lifestyle protocols.
  • Thyroid & Lipids – Periodic monitoring and dose optimisation.
  • Preventive Health – Annual screening, vaccination and counselling.

Conditions & Care

Consult for timely diagnosis, clear treatment plans and long-term health maintenance tailored to your goals.

  • Acute: viral fever, flu, sore throat, bronchitis, gastritis.
  • Chronic: diabetes, high BP, cholesterol, thyroid, obesity.
  • Preventive: executive health check, cardiac risk assessment.
  • Medication review and second opinion.
