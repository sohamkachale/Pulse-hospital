About Dr. Ulhas Pote

Dr. Ulhas Pote is a Laparoscopic / IVF consultant with experience in his fields from different parts of India and has obtained a Diploma from GERMANY in Laparoscopy. His experience is of 9 years and has been trained in his field by the Pioneers of his field both from India and outside.

Professional Skills

Minimally invasive gynecologic surgery, ultrasound-guided fertility workflows, individualized stimulation plans, post-operative recovery optimization, and patient education for informed decisions.

Education & Credentials

Structured training with international exposure in laparoscopy and fertility science; outcome-oriented and protocol-driven practice.

  • Diploma in Laparoscopy – Germany (minimally invasive gynecology).
  • Advanced IVF Exposure – Training with national & international pioneers.
  • Focused Experience – 6 years in gynecology, obstetrics, laparoscopy & fertility.
