Urology Services

01

Kidney Stone Management

Kidney stones can cause severe pain and discomfort, which is why we offer fast and effective treatment options. Our specialists use laser technology, ESWL lithotripsy, PCNL, and mini-PCNL procedures to remove stones safely. Whether you have small stones or complex cases, we provide personalized treatment plans and guidance to prevent recurrence.

02

Prostate Care & Treatment

Prostate-related issues are common as men age, and early diagnosis makes all the difference. We offer complete care for enlarged prostate (BPH), including laser prostate surgery, TURP procedures, and advanced screening tests. Our team ensures compassionate care while helping patients overcome discomfort, frequent urination, and prostate-related health challenges.

03

Urinary Tract Infection (UTI) Treatment

Recurrent UTIs can affect daily life and overall health. Our specialists identify the root cause using advanced diagnostics and offer customized treatment based on age, gender, and symptoms. Whether it’s pain, burning, fever, or discomfort, we provide timely relief and long-term solutions to prevent repeated infections.

04

Male Sexual & Reproductive Health (Andrology)

We provide dedicated care for male reproductive health issues such as erectile dysfunction, infertility, and hormonal imbalance. Our experts ensure a private, judgment-free environment where you can openly discuss your concerns. With modern therapies and diagnostic tools, we help restore confidence and improve quality of life.

05

Pediatric Urology

Children require special attention, and our pediatric urology team is trained to diagnose and treat urinary issues in young patients. We manage conditions like bedwetting, undescended testicles, vesicoureteral reflux, and congenital kidney problems with gentle care and advanced solutions.

06

Urology Surgeries & Procedures

We perform a wide range of minimally invasive and laparoscopic surgeries to ensure quicker recovery and minimal discomfort. Our services include laser circumcision, hydrocele surgery, vasectomy, urethral stricture treatment, and laparoscopic urology surgeries. Every procedure follows strict safety standards and modern surgical protocols.

Frequently Asked Questions

The best urologist in Tathawade is available at Pulse Multispeciality Hospital, specializing in kidney stones, prostate care, and urinary tract disorders. Our experienced doctors combine advanced surgical skills with personalized treatment plans, ensuring safe, effective care and excellent patient outcomes for all age groups.

Male infertility treatment depends on the underlying cause, such as low sperm count, hormonal imbalance, or reproductive tract issues. Treatments may include medication, hormonal therapy, surgical correction, or assisted reproductive techniques. Early consultation with a urologist ensures accurate diagnosis and increases the chances of successful conception.

Kidney stones often cause severe pain in the lower back or sides, blood in urine, nausea, vomiting, and frequent urination. Early detection through ultrasound or CT scans ensures prompt treatment. Modern urology techniques provide effective stone removal with minimal discomfort and quick recovery.

Kidney stones can be treated with medications, lifestyle changes, and advanced procedures. Options include ESWL lithotripsy, laser stone removal, and PCNL surgeries. Treatment depends on the stone size, location, and symptoms, ensuring safe, effective, and minimally invasive solutions to relieve pain and prevent recurrence.

UTIs occur when bacteria enter the urinary tract, causing pain, burning sensation, frequent urination, and sometimes fever. Risk factors include dehydration, poor hygiene, urinary blockages, and weakened immunity. Early diagnosis and targeted antibiotics or treatment prevent recurrence and complications, ensuring complete urinary health recovery.

Urinary blockage may result from stones, prostate enlargement, or strictures. Treatment options include medications, minimally invasive procedures, or surgery depending on the cause. Early diagnosis by a urologist ensures effective treatment, prevents complications, and restores normal urinary function safely.

Prevent kidney stones by staying hydrated, reducing salt and oxalate-rich foods, limiting processed foods, and maintaining a balanced diet. Regular exercise and weight management also help. A urologist may recommend supplements or medications to reduce recurrence risk based on stone type and patient history.
