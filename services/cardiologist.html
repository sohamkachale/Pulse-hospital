Cardiology in Tathawade, PCMC — 24×7 Emergency & Expert Heart Care

Advanced diagnosis, clear treatment plans, and round-the-clock emergency support for chest pain, hypertension, cholesterol issues, palpitations, and heart failure—delivered by experienced cardiologists serving Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Conditions We Treat

  • Chest pain, breathlessness, palpitations, dizziness, ankle swelling
  • Hypertension, high cholesterol, diabetes-related heart risk
  • Coronary artery disease, stable/unstable angina, post-heart-attack follow-up
  • Heart failure, valve disease, cardiomyopathy, arrhythmias
  • Preventive cardiology for strong family history or high risk scores

On-Site Diagnostics (Fast & Accurate)

  • ECG and cardiac enzymes for acute chest pain triage
  • 2D Echo for structure & function, TMT for exercise-induced ischemia
  • Holter (24–48 hr) rhythm monitoring & ambulatory BP monitoring
  • X-ray & lab panels supporting comprehensive cardiac evaluation

Treatments & Ongoing Management

  • Evidence-based medication plans with regular BP/lipid/glucose review
  • Heart-healthy diet guidance suited to Indian home meals
  • Return-to-work / activity targets and rehab-linked exercise plans
  • Heart-attack stabilization in 24×7 ER & ICU; rapid cath-lab referral when indicated
  • Cashless support wherever applicable; coordinated discharge & follow-up

When to Visit Emergency (Do Not Delay)

  • New or crushing chest pain, pain radiating to arm/jaw/back, cold sweats
  • Sudden severe breathlessness, fainting, fast/irregular heartbeat
  • BP > 180/110 with headache/blurred vision or neuro symptoms

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • 24×7 emergency & ICU with certified cardiac-care protocols
  • Experienced cardiology panel and multi-specialty backup
  • On-site tests → quicker diagnosis, fewer hops, faster relief
  • Patient-first, transparent, insurance-assisted processes
