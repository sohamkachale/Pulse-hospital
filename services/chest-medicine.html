Chest Medicine (Pulmonology) — Asthma, COPD, Infection & Sleep Breathing Care

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment for respiratory symptoms—breathlessness, cough, wheeze, chest pain, snoring and poor sleep. We combine protocol-based diagnosis, inhaler education, day-care procedures and coordinated ICU support for safe, faster recovery.

Conditions We Treat

  • Asthma, allergic rhinitis, exercise-induced bronchospasm
  • COPD, chronic bronchitis, emphysema, frequent exacerbations
  • Pneumonia, post-viral cough, bronchiectasis
  • Tuberculosis (pulmonary & pleural) and post-TB airway disease
  • Interstitial lung disease (ILD), hypersensitivity pneumonitis
  • Sleep apnea (OSA), snoring, daytime sleepiness
  • Pleural effusion, pneumothorax; smoking-related lung problems

Diagnostics & Tests

  • Pulmonary Function Test (PFT) / Spirometry with bronchodilator reversibility
  • 6-Minute Walk Test (6MWT) and pulse oximetry trend
  • Chest X-ray; HRCT thorax for ILD/bronchiectasis as indicated
  • ABG analysis; allergy testing (case-based); sputum AFB/GeneXpert for TB
  • Polysomnography / home sleep study for suspected sleep apnea
  • Diagnostic & therapeutic bronchoscopy when required

Treatments & Procedures

  • Personalised asthma/COPD plans with correct inhaler-spacer technique
  • Nebulisation, oxygen therapy, NIV/CPAP (as advised)
  • Bronchoscopy: airway toileting, BAL sampling; foreign body evaluation
  • Thoracentesis for pleural effusion; intercostal drain insertion (ICD) when needed
  • Anti-TB therapy per national guidelines; ILD management with multidisciplinary input
  • Smoking cessation counselling and vaccination advice (flu, pneumococcal)

When to Visit Emergency

  • Severe breathlessness, blue lips, speaking in broken words
  • High fever with chest pain, confusion, or low oxygen readings
  • Sudden chest tightness with rapid worsening cough/wheeze
  • Acute pleuritic pain after cough/strain (suspected pneumothorax)

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced pulmonology team with PFT, bronchoscopy and imaging pathways
  • 24×7 emergency and ICU for severe exacerbations and infections
  • Patient education on inhalers, triggers and relapse prevention
  • Insurance/TPA assistance and clear follow-up schedules
