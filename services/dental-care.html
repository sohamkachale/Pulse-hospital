Dental Care — Preventive, Restorative & Cosmetic Dentistry

Comprehensive oral care for pain relief, functional bite, and confident smiles. From routine checkups and cleanings to root canals, crowns, and smile makeovers—our dentists provide evidence-based, gentle treatment for all age groups.

Concerns We Treat

  • Toothache, sensitivity, decayed or broken tooth, fractured fillings
  • Gum bleeding/swelling, bad breath, loose teeth, periodontal disease
  • Missing teeth, chewing difficulty, ill-fitting dentures
  • Malaligned teeth, spacing/crowding, bite issues (children & adults)
  • Stained, chipped or uneven teeth; gummy smile; smile enhancement needs

Diagnostics & Preventive Care

  • Intraoral examination with dental X-rays when indicated
  • Scaling & polishing; oral hygiene instruction and diet counselling
  • Fluoride application and pit-and-fissure sealants for kids
  • Screening for oral precancerous lesions in tobacco users

Treatments We Provide

  • Fillings & Restorations: Tooth-coloured composites and inlays/onlays
  • Root Canal Treatment (RCT): Single/multi-visit RCT with crown placement
  • Crowns & Bridges: Metal-ceramic, zirconia, full-ceramic options
  • Extractions & Minor Surgery: Simple/impacted tooth removal as advised
  • Gum Therapy: Deep cleaning, root planing, periodontal care
  • Prosthodontics: Complete/partial dentures; implant referrals where needed
  • Orthodontics: Braces and clear aligner planning for bite correction
  • Cosmetic Dentistry: Teeth whitening, veneers, contouring & smile design
  • Pediatric Dentistry: Child-friendly fillings, pulpotomy/pulpectomy, habit counselling

Dental Emergencies — Visit Early

  • Severe toothache, facial swelling, fever with dental infection
  • Knocked-out or fractured tooth after injury (bring the tooth in milk/saline)
  • Uncontrolled gum bleeding, broken crowns/bridges causing pain

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced dental team with X-ray support and sterilisation protocols
  • Quality materials, conservative dentistry and painless techniques
  • Clear estimates, multiple crown/braces options, and post-treatment guidance
  • Coordination with medical specialties for medically complex patients
