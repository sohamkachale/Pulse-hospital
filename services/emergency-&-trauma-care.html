Our Emergency & Trauma Care Services

01

Emergency Medical
Care

We provide round-the-clock emergency medical services for heart attacks, strokes, breathing difficulty, seizures, high fever, and sudden illness. Our doctors act immediately to stabilise the patient, perform a quick diagnosis, and begin treatment without delay, ensuring safe and effective emergency care.

02

Accident & Trauma
Care

Our trauma team delivers rapid care for road accidents, fractures, major injuries, wounds, and internal bleeding. We use advanced emergency systems to stabilise patients early, prevent complications, and provide coordinated treatment with specialists for reliable accident and trauma management in Tathawade and PCMC.

03

Critical Care & ICU Support

Our ICU handles life-threatening emergencies with skilled doctors, ventilator support, advanced monitoring, and 24×7 specialist supervision. We respond immediately to bring the patient under safe critical care, offering precise treatment required in severe conditions like stroke, shock, organ failure or severe infection.

04

Rapid Diagnostics Available 24/7

We offer immediate access to CT scans, X-rays, ultrasounds, ECGs, and emergency blood tests at any hour. Fast diagnostics enable quicker decisions, accurate treatment, and better outcomes in emergencies, ensuring patients get the right care without waiting.

05

Pediatric Emergency Care

Our pediatric emergency team treats infants and children with urgent conditions such as seizures, breathing trouble, high fever, fractures, or injuries. We respond quickly while ensuring comfort and safety, providing specialised care tailored to young patients when every moment matters.
