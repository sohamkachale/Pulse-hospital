Endocrinology & Diabetes — Sugar Control, Thyroid & Hormone Health

Specialised care for diabetes and hormonal disorders with individualised targets, medication/insulin plans, nutrition counselling and complication screening. We help you reach stable sugars, healthy weight and better long-term outcomes.

Conditions We Treat

  • Type 1 & Type 2 Diabetes, Prediabetes, Gestational Diabetes
  • Thyroid disorders: Hypothyroidism, Hyperthyroidism, Thyroid nodules
  • PCOS (irregular periods, acne, excess hair, fertility planning)
  • Obesity & metabolic syndrome, fatty liver–related metabolic risk
  • Osteoporosis & vitamin D/calcium imbalance
  • Adrenal disorders (Addison’s, Cushing’s), Pituitary disorders
  • Hypogonadism, puberty & growth concerns (as appropriate)

Diagnostics & Monitoring

  • HbA1c, fasting/post-meal sugars, lipid profile, kidney profile
  • Thyroid profile (TSH, T3, T4), thyroid ultrasound when indicated
  • CGM (Continuous Glucose Monitoring) / glucose profiles
  • Neuropathy screening, urine microalbumin, retinal screening referrals
  • DXA scan referrals for bone density; hormonal assays as advised

Treatments & Ongoing Management

  • Personalised diet & activity plans suitable for Indian meals & routines
  • Oral antidiabetics, basal/bolus insulin titration; education on hypoglycaemia
  • Insulin pump/CGM pathway where appropriate; sick-day rules
  • Thyroid replacement or antithyroid therapy with periodic monitoring
  • PCOS care: cycle regulation, weight management, fertility coordination
  • Bone health: vitamin D/calcium optimisation, osteoporosis therapy
  • Annual complication screening & vaccination advice

When to Visit Emergency / Urgent Care

  • Very high sugars with vomiting/drowsiness, fruity breath (DKA suspicion)
  • Recurrent low sugars with confusion or seizures
  • Chest pain, sudden weakness, vision loss, severe dehydration

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Endocrinology-led protocols with diabetes educator & diet counselling
  • CGM-guided titration, complication screening and coordinated referrals
  • 24×7 emergency & ICU support for acute metabolic events
  • Clear follow-ups, practical targets, and patient-first guidance
