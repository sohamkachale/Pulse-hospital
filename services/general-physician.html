General Physician in Tathawade, PCMC — Complete Adult Care

Your first stop for ethical, evidence-based primary care. We treat common illnesses, manage chronic conditions, and guide preventive health—serving Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Conditions We Treat

  • Fever, cough/cold, flu, throat & ear infections
  • Headache, migraine, body aches, fatigue, dizziness
  • Acidity, gastritis, IBS symptoms, constipation, abdominal pain
  • Urine infections, dehydration, basic kidney function screening
  • Allergies, minor skin rashes, lifestyle-related complaints

Chronic Disease Management

  • Diabetes care: sugar profile review, medication titration, diet counselling
  • Hypertension control: BP monitoring, risk assessment, home log reviews
  • Thyroid disorders: TSH/Free T4 interpretation & long-term follow-up
  • Dyslipidemia: lipid profile optimisation & cardio-metabolic counselling
  • Obesity & lifestyle: weight management plans and habit coaching

On-Site Investigations & Screening

  • Baseline tests: CBC, LFT, KFT, urine routine, fasting sugar, HbA1c
  • ECG for chest discomfort; referral for 2D Echo/TMT if indicated
  • Thyroid & vitamins: TSH, B12, D3 with pragmatic supplementation
  • Infection workup: malaria, dengue, typhoid; X-ray when needed
  • Age- and risk-appropriate annual health checkups

Treatment Approach

  • Guideline-driven prescriptions with the lowest effective doses
  • Clear do’s & don’ts, red-flag symptoms, and home-care instructions
  • Medication reconciliation to avoid duplications/interactions
  • Timely referrals to Cardiology, Neurology, ENT, Surgery, Physiotherapy
  • Structured follow-ups with simple, trackable goals

When to Visit Emergency (Do Not Delay)

  • High fever with confusion, persistent vomiting, severe dehydration
  • Chest pain, sudden breathlessness, fainting, new neurological deficits
  • Very high BP or sugar readings with alarming symptoms

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced physicians, ethical care, and transparent communication
  • On-site diagnostics & pharmacy for faster decisions
  • 24×7 Emergency & ICU with multi-specialty backup
  • Insurance support and coordinated follow-ups
