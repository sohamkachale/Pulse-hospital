General Surgery & Minimal Access — Safe, Precise & Faster Recovery

Laparoscopic and open surgeries performed by experienced surgeons with ER/ICU backup, standardized safety checklists, and enhanced recovery protocols. Serving Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Conditions We Treat

  • Gallstones, acute cholecystitis; appendicitis
  • Hernias: inguinal, umbilical, ventral, incisional
  • Piles, fissure, fistula, pilonidal sinus
  • Thyroid nodules/goiter; benign breast lumps
  • GI emergencies: obstruction, perforation, peritonitis

Diagnostics

  • Ultrasound abdomen, X-ray, CT scan as indicated
  • Pre-op workup: CBC, LFT/RFT, coagulation, ECG, chest X-ray

Procedures

  • Laparoscopic cholecystectomy & appendectomy
  • Hernia repair (lap/open) with mesh — TAPP/TEP
  • Laser treatment for piles, fissure, fistula
  • Thyroid & breast surgeries with oncologic referral where needed
  • Trauma & emergency abdominal surgery

Enhanced Recovery

  • ERAS protocols — less pain, early ambulation, shorter stay
  • Physiotherapy, nutrition, and wound-care counselling

When to Visit Emergency

  • Severe abdominal pain, vomiting, fever, or inability to pass gas/stool
  • Sudden groin swelling with pain (strangulated hernia)
  • Rectal bleeding, black stools, or persistent vomiting

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced surgical team with 24×7 Emergency & ICU
  • Laparoscopy-first approach for quicker recovery
  • Transparent counselling, insurance assistance, planned follow-up
