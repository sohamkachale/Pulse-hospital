Hematology & Bone Marrow Transplant (BMT) — Comprehensive Blood Disorder Care

Specialized diagnosis and treatment for anemia, platelet problems, bleeding/clotting disorders, and blood cancers (leukemia, lymphoma, myeloma). Multidisciplinary care with 24×7 emergency and ICU support for patients across Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Conditions We Treat

  • Anemia (iron, B12/folate deficiency), thalassemia, sickle cell disease
  • Low/high platelets, ITP, bone marrow failure, aplastic anemia
  • Bleeding disorders (hemophilia, von Willebrand disease), clotting disorders (DVT/PE)
  • Leukemias (ALL/AML/CLL/CML), lymphomas (Hodgkin/Non-Hodgkin), multiple myeloma
  • Myeloproliferative & myelodysplastic syndromes, hypersplenism

On-Site Diagnostics

  • Complete blood count (CBC), peripheral smear, reticulocyte count
  • Iron studies, B12/folate, Coombs test, LDH
  • Coagulation profile (PT/INR, aPTT), D-dimer, thrombophilia work-up
  • Bone marrow aspiration/biopsy, flow cytometry, immunophenotyping
  • Cytogenetics & molecular tests (e.g., BCR-ABL, JAK2, CALR), HLA typing for BMT

Treatments & Day-Care Services

  • Evidence-based chemotherapy protocols and targeted therapy (e.g., TKIs)
  • Immunotherapy & monoclonal antibodies as indicated
  • Transfusion services (PRBC, platelets, plasma) with strict safety checks
  • Anticoagulation clinic for DVT/PE and cardiac-related clot care
  • Iron chelation, growth factors, infection prophylaxis & vaccination plans

Bone Marrow Transplant (BMT) Program

  • Autologous and allogeneic BMT evaluation with donor search & HLA matching
  • Pre-transplant work-up, conditioning regimens, and inpatient isolation care
  • GvHD prevention, monitoring, and management by trained team
  • Post-transplant follow-up: infection surveillance, nutrition, and revaccination schedule

When to Visit Emergency

  • High fever > 38°C in a patient on chemotherapy or with low counts
  • Uncontrolled bleeding, sudden large bruises, black/tarry stools
  • Severe pallor, dizziness, chest pain, or breathlessness
  • Suspected clot: limb swelling/pain, sudden chest pain, or coughing blood

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • 24×7 Emergency & ICU with hematology protocols and rapid triage
  • Experienced hematologists with multidisciplinary support (medicine, oncology, ICU)
  • On-site diagnostics & day-care chemo for faster, coordinated care
  • Ethical, transparent treatment with insurance assistance & guided counseling
