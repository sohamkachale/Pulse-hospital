HPB & Surgical Gastroenterology — Liver, Pancreas, Gallbladder & GI Surgery

Comprehensive evaluation and advanced surgical care for gallbladder stones, jaundice/obstructive cholestasis, pancreatitis, liver tumors/cysts, bile duct strictures, GI cancers, hernias, and complex abdominal conditions. Laparoscopic and open surgery options with 24×7 emergency and ICU support for patients across Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Conditions We Treat

  • Gallbladder stones, acute cholecystitis, biliary colic, CBD stones
  • Jaundice due to bile duct stones/stricture, cholangitis
  • Acute & chronic pancreatitis, pancreatic pseudocyst, pancreatic tumors
  • Liver tumors (HCC, metastases), cysts, hydatid disease
  • Esophageal, gastric, colorectal cancers; inflammatory bowel disease
  • All types of hernia (inguinal, umbilical, ventral, incisional)

On-Site Diagnostics

  • Ultrasound abdomen, X-ray, CT/MRI abdomen with liver/pancreas protocols
  • LFT, pancreatic enzymes (amylase/lipase), CBC, coagulation profile
  • MRCP for bile duct evaluation; endoscopy/colonoscopy as indicated
  • Tumor markers (AFP, CA 19-9, CEA) where clinically relevant

Treatments & Procedures

  • Laparoscopic cholecystectomy for gallstones & acute cholecystitis
  • ERCP for CBD stones/strictures; stenting for obstructive jaundice
  • Pancreatic necrosectomy, pseudocyst drainage (endoscopic/surgical)
  • Liver resections, cyst surgeries; portal biliopathy/stricture management
  • Upper GI (esophagus/stomach) and colorectal cancer surgeries
  • Laparoscopic/open hernia repair (TAPP/TEP/mesh) with rapid recovery plans

Enhanced Recovery & Day-Care

  • ERAS protocols for faster mobilization, less pain, and shorter stay
  • Day-care endoscopy/colonoscopy, diagnostic & therapeutic procedures
  • Nutrition counseling and physiotherapy-guided rehabilitation

When to Visit Emergency

  • Severe upper-abdominal pain with fever/vomiting or jaundice
  • Acute abdomen with persistent vomiting or abdominal distension
  • Blood in stool/vomit, black tarry stools, or sudden rectal bleeding
  • Post-operative severe pain, fever, uncontrolled vomiting, or wound issues

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced HPB & GI surgeons with 24×7 Emergency & ICU backup
  • Advanced imaging, ERCP/endoscopy, and laparoscopy under one roof
  • Standardized safety checklists, infection control, and pain management
  • Insurance assistance, transparent counseling, and coordinated follow-up
