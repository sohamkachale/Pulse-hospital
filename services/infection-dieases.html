Infectious Diseases — Expert Fever & Infection Care with Rapid Diagnosis

From seasonal fevers to complex infections, our team delivers timely diagnosis, evidence-based treatment, and infection-control guided care. We support patients across Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad with 24×7 emergency and ICU backup.

Conditions We Treat

  • Acute undifferentiated fever, viral fevers, influenza, COVID-19
  • Dengue, malaria, chikungunya, typhoid, leptospirosis
  • Pneumonia, bronchitis, sinusitis, sore throat (bacterial/viral)
  • Gastroenteritis, food poisoning, hepatitis A/E related infections
  • UTI, pyelonephritis; skin/soft tissue infections (cellulitis/abscess)
  • Tuberculosis (pulmonary & extra-pulmonary) with DOTS coordination
  • Sepsis & bloodstream infections; hospital-acquired infections

On-Site Diagnostics

  • Rapid fever panels: Dengue NS1/IgM, Malaria (RDT/smear), Typhoid tests
  • RT-PCR/Antigen for influenza & COVID-19; throat/nasal swabs
  • CBC, CRP, LFT, RFT, electrolytes, procalcitonin for sepsis work-up
  • Blood/urine/stool cultures & sensitivity, AFB smear/GeneXpert for TB
  • X-ray chest; USG/CT/MRI as indicated for deep-seated infections

Treatments & Day-Care Services

  • Evidence-based antivirals/antibiotics with antimicrobial stewardship
  • IV fluids, antipyretics, platelet monitoring & supportive care for dengue
  • Oxygen therapy, nebulization, and isolation protocols where required
  • Vaccinations: influenza, hepatitis, typhoid, tetanus; pre-travel counseling
  • Inpatient care for pneumonia/sepsis with ICU backup and early mobilization

Infection Control & Prevention

  • Standard precautions, hand-hygiene, and ward-level isolation practices
  • Antibiotic policy with culture-guided de-escalation to reduce resistance
  • Patient education on hydration, mosquito protection, and safe food/water

When to Visit Emergency

  • High fever > 38.5°C with severe headache, persistent vomiting, or confusion
  • Breathlessness, chest pain, low oxygen, or seizures
  • Bleeding gums/nose, black stools, very low urine output, or severe weakness

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • 24×7 Emergency & ICU with sepsis bundles and escalation pathways
  • Rapid diagnosis under one roof: lab, radiology, and fever panels
  • Experienced physicians with strict infection-control protocols
  • Transparent communication, insurance assistance, and planned follow-ups
