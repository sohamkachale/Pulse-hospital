Interventional Radiology — Minimally Invasive, Image-Guided Care

Pin-hole procedures done under ultrasound/CT/fluoroscopy guidance for faster recovery, less pain, and shorter hospital stay. Dedicated IR suite with 24×7 emergency and ICU support for patients across Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

What We Treat

  • Peripheral arterial disease, non-healing ulcers, limb ischemia
  • Uterine fibroids, adenomyosis, postpartum hemorrhage
  • Varicose veins, venous insufficiency, DVT sequelae
  • Obstructive jaundice (biliary), urinary obstruction (PCN/ureteric)
  • Benign/malignant fluid collections, abscesses, lung/pleural effusions
  • Onco IR: TACE/TARE referral pathway, tumor ablation (as indicated)

On-Site Imaging

  • Digital subtraction angiography (DSA) suite
  • Ultrasound with Doppler; CT & MRI tie-ups on campus
  • Cath-lab coordination for complex vascular cases

Procedures We Offer

  • Angiography, angioplasty & stenting (peripheral/renal/visceral)
  • Uterine artery embolization (UAE), bleeding control embolization
  • Endovenous laser ablation (EVLA) for varicose veins
  • USG/CT-guided biopsies & FNAC (liver, lung, bone, nodes)
  • Drainages (pigtail), biliary/urinary stenting, nephrostomy (PCN)
  • PICC lines, chemoport placement, gastrostomy (PEG) as indicated

When to Visit Emergency

  • Active internal bleeding, traumatic vascular injury
  • Critical limb pain/color change suggesting acute ischemia
  • Obstructive jaundice with fever/chills (suspected cholangitis)

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced interventional radiologists with anesthesia & ICU backup
  • Minimally invasive options → less pain, quicker recovery
  • Ethical counseling, cost transparency, insurance assistance
