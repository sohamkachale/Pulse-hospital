Mental Health — Psychiatry & Psychology with Compassion

Confidential, stigma-free care for emotional, behavioral, and psychological concerns. Integrated team of psychiatrists and psychologists offering therapy, medication management, and family support for patients from Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Concerns We Address

  • Depression, anxiety, panic, OCD, phobias
  • Bipolar disorder, psychosis, schizophrenia (maintenance programs)
  • Child & adolescent issues: hyperactivity, learning/behavioral challenges
  • Sleep problems, burnout, work-stress, grief
  • Substance use: alcohol, tobacco, drugs — de-addiction plans
  • Perinatal mental health, geriatric mental health

Evaluation & Screening

  • Detailed clinical interview & mental status examination
  • Standardized scales (PHQ-9, GAD-7, YMRS, MOCA and others as indicated)
  • Medical rule-outs (thyroid, B12, vitamin D, sleep apnea referral)

Treatments & Therapies

  • Personalized medication plans with close follow-up
  • Psychotherapy: CBT, DBT skills, supportive/couple/family therapy
  • De-addiction: detox, relapse-prevention, peer/family programs
  • Sleep hygiene, stress management, lifestyle & nutrition guidance
  • Day-care observation; ECT referral pathway when clinically indicated

When to Visit Emergency

  • Thoughts of self-harm or suicide, severe agitation or violence risk
  • Confusion, hallucinations, acute intoxication/overdose
  • Sudden inability to care for self or toddler/elder safety concerns

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Compassionate, confidential care with evidence-based protocols
  • Integrated support: psychotherapy, medication, family counselling
  • 24×7 Emergency & ICU backup for crisis stabilization
