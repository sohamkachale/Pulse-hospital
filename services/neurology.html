Neurology in Tathawade, PCMC — Stroke, Epilepsy, Migraine & Nerve Care

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment for brain, spine, nerve, and muscle disorders. Our neurology team blends accurate diagnosis with clear plans, backed by 24×7 Emergency, ICU, and rehabilitation support for safer recovery and long-term control.

Conditions We Treat

  • Stroke & TIA (mini-stroke), carotid disease, post-stroke rehabilitation
  • Epilepsy & seizures, first-seizure workup, medication optimisation
  • Headache & migraine, trigeminal neuralgia, cluster headache
  • Back/neck pain with nerve compression, radiculopathy, sciatica
  • Peripheral neuropathy (diabetic/deficiency-related), numbness, burning feet
  • Parkinson’s disease & movement disorders, dystonia, tremor
  • Dementia, memory loss, cognitive decline
  • Multiple sclerosis & demyelinating disorders
  • Myasthenia gravis & neuromuscular disorders
  • Vertigo/balance disorders; CNS infections (meningitis/encephalitis)

Neurology Diagnostics

  • EEG for seizures/encephalopathy; video-EEG when indicated
  • Nerve Conduction Velocity (NCV) & EMG for neuropathy and radiculopathy
  • MRI/CT brain & spine; carotid/vertebral Doppler (as advised)
  • Laboratory panels: metabolic, autoimmune, infectious workups
  • Lumbar puncture (CSF analysis) when clinically required

Treatments & Care Pathways

  • Stroke: Rapid triage (FAST), thrombolysis eligibility assessment, BP/glucose targets, and stroke-unit pathway; early rehab planning
  • Epilepsy: Seizure type classification, antiseizure medicines, rescue plans, driving/fitness counselling
  • Migraine & Headache: Acute and preventive regimens, trigger management, lifestyle & sleep hygiene
  • Neuropathy/Back-Neck Pain: Cause correction, neuropathic pain protocol, physio-guided rehab
  • Movement Disorders: Medication titration, botulinum toxin for dystonia/spasticity (case-based)
  • MS/Autoimmune: Immunotherapy pathways and vaccination guidance (as indicated)

Rehabilitation & Prevention

  • Post-stroke rehab: physiotherapy, speech/swallow therapy, occupational therapy
  • Fall-risk reduction, home safety, caregiver training
  • Risk-factor control: diabetes, BP, lipids, weight, smoking cessation

When to Seek Emergency Care

  • Facial droop, arm/leg weakness, slurred speech, sudden vision loss (possible stroke)
  • Seizure > 5 minutes or repeated seizures without recovery
  • “Worst headache of life”, new severe headache with fever/neck stiffness
  • Head injury with loss of consciousness or worsening drowsiness

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced neurology team with 24×7 ER and ICU backup
  • Access to EEG, NCV/EMG and advanced neuro-imaging pathways
  • Integrated rehab and multidisciplinary care for better outcomes
  • Transparent counselling, medication safety checks, and follow-up plans
