Neurosurgery Services for Brain & Spine

01

Brain Tumor Surgery

At Pulse Hospital, our brain tumor specialists provide advanced surgical care using minimally invasive techniques and precise navigation systems. We treat various tumors, including gliomas, meningiomas, and pituitary tumors, ensuring safety and optimal outcomes. Our team prioritizes preserving neurological function and enhancing post-surgery recovery for every patient.

02

Spine Surgery and Spinal Cord Injury Treatment

Pulse Hospital offers comprehensive spine care for conditions like herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and traumatic spinal cord injuries. Utilizing minimally invasive techniques, our expert surgeons reduce recovery time, pain, and complications. Personalized rehabilitation programs help patients regain mobility, strength, and independence while ensuring long-term spine health and improved quality of life.

03

Pediatric Neurosurgery

Our pediatric neurosurgery team at Pulse Hospital provides specialized care for children with neurological disorders, including congenital conditions, brain tumors, hydrocephalus, and epilepsy. Using gentle techniques and advanced surgical methods, we ensure safety, comfort, and effective outcomes, creating a child-friendly environment that supports families throughout diagnosis, treatment, and recovery.

04

Epilepsy and Neurological Disorder Treatments

Pulse Hospital delivers expert care for epilepsy and other neurological disorders with advanced diagnostics and surgical interventions. Treatments include temporal lobe resection, vagus nerve stimulation, and responsive neurostimulation. Our goal is to reduce seizure frequency, enhance independence, and improve overall quality of life through personalized, evidence-based treatment plans for every patient.

05

Stroke and Emergency Neurosurgery Care

Pulse Hospital provides rapid, 24/7 emergency neurosurgical care for strokes, head injuries, and brain hemorrhages. Our neuro ICU and advanced monitoring systems ensure immediate stabilization, precise intervention, and comprehensive post-emergency treatment. We focus on reducing complications, improving survival rates, and supporting patients and families during every critical step of recovery.
