Oncology in Tathawade, PCMC — Chemotherapy Day-Care, Targeted & Immunotherapy

Integrated cancer care with accurate staging, multidisciplinary tumor board planning, and safe delivery of systemic therapy. Our team coordinates medical oncology, surgery and radiation referrals, plus pain and palliative support—backed by 24×7 Emergency and ICU.

Cancers We Evaluate & Manage

  • Breast, lung, head & neck, gastrointestinal (esophagus, stomach, colon, rectum)
  • Hepato-pancreato-biliary (liver, pancreas), gallbladder & bile duct cancers
  • Gynecologic (ovary, uterus, cervix), genitourinary (kidney, bladder, prostate)
  • Hematologic (lymphoma, leukemia, myeloma)
  • Bone/soft-tissue tumors; supportive care for advanced/metastatic disease

Workup & Staging

  • Biopsy/FNAC with histopathology & immunohistochemistry (IHC)
  • Staging imaging: USG/CT/MRI; PET-CT referral when indicated
  • Tumor markers and molecular profiling for targeted therapy decisions
  • Multidisciplinary tumor board for individualized treatment plans

Treatments We Provide

  • Chemotherapy Day-Care: Protocol-based regimens with pre-medication and infusion safety checks
  • Targeted Therapy: Molecular-guided drugs for selected mutations
  • Immunotherapy: Checkpoint inhibitors in eligible cancers
  • Hormonal Therapy: Breast/prostate and other hormone-sensitive tumors
  • Central line care (PICC/port) and chemo-related toxicity management
  • Surgery and radiation therapy coordinated with partner teams when needed

Supportive, Pain & Palliative Care

  • Anti-emetic, growth-factor, and infection-prevention protocols
  • Pain relief pathways, nerve blocks (via pain team) where required
  • Nutritional counselling, physiotherapy, lymphedema guidance
  • Psychosocial support and end-of-life care planning when appropriate

When to Visit Emergency

  • Fever ≥ 38°C during/after chemotherapy, uncontrolled vomiting/diarrhoea
  • Bleeding, severe breathlessness, chest pain, sudden weakness or confusion
  • Dehydration, poor oral intake, or rapidly worsening pain

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Protocol-driven oncology with chemo day-care and ICU backup
  • Personalised plans via tumor board and molecular profiling
  • Integrated pain, nutrition, and rehabilitation support
  • Insurance/TPA assistance and counselling for caregivers
