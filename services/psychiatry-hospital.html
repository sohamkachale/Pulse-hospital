Psychiatry

Mental health deserves the same care, attention, and compassion as physical health and at the Psychiatry Department of Pulse Multispeciality Hospital in Tathawade, PCMC, we make sure every patient feels understood and supported.

With a team of experienced psychiatrists, advanced assessment tools, and a warm, healing environment, we are committed to helping individuals regain emotional balance, clarity, and confidence. Whether you're facing stress, trauma, or long-standing mental health concerns, you’ll find safe, confidential, and patient-focused care here.

Services

Our Psychiatry Services

01

Depression Treatment

We provide specialised care for all forms of depression, whether mild, moderate, or severe. Our approach includes a thorough psychological evaluation, counselling sessions, lifestyle guidance, and medication when needed. The goal is to help you regain motivation, emotional stability, and the ability to enjoy life again. Our experienced psychiatrist works closely with you to understand triggers, patterns, and long-term solutions.

02

Anxiety & Panic Disorder Management

Anxiety can feel overwhelming, but with the right treatment, you can take control again. Our therapists and psychiatrists combine counselling, cognitive behavioural therapy, and personalised treatment plans to help you manage symptoms like worry, fear, and panic attacks. We guide you in understanding your anxiety triggers and building coping strategies for long-term relief.

03

Stress Management & Emotional Well-Being

If stress is affecting your daily life, sleep, or work performance, our stress management program can help. We provide proven therapeutic approaches, practical lifestyle recommendations, and relaxation techniques to help you regain calmness. Our experts support you in identifying stress patterns and developing healthier ways to manage daily pressures.

04

OCD & Phobia Treatment

We offer structured therapy programs for obsessive–compulsive disorder and different types of phobias. Using evidence-based methods such as CBT and exposure therapy, we help patients reduce compulsive behaviours, challenge negative thought patterns, and regain control over their lives. Treatment is always done gradually, respectfully, and at a pace you are comfortable with.

05

Bipolar Disorder Treatment

Bipolar disorder requires accurate diagnosis and long-term support. Our psychiatrist creates a customised treatment plan that includes medication management, counselling, mood monitoring, and lifestyle adjustments. We aim to help patients achieve stability, prevent relapses, and improve overall emotional quality of life.

06

Schizophrenia & Psychotic Disorders Care

For individuals experiencing hallucinations, delusions, or severe behavioural changes, we offer a supportive and structured treatment system. Our team provides psychiatric evaluation, medication management, counselling, and family education. With proper guidance, patients can lead healthier and more stable lives.

07

Addiction Psychiatry

Whether it’s alcohol, smoking, or drug dependency, we offer confidential and supportive addiction treatment programs. Our psychiatrist evaluates both psychological and physical factors contributing to addiction and designs a personalised recovery plan. Therapy, detox assistance, relapse prevention, and lifestyle counselling all come together to help patients regain control of their lives.

08

Child & Adolescent Psychiatry

Children and teenagers face unique emotional and behavioural challenges. We provide age-appropriate counselling and treatment for issues such as ADHD, anxiety, learning difficulties, autism-related concerns, anger issues, and emotional instability. Our approach is gentle, structured, and family-inclusive.

09

Geriatric Psychiatry

Mental health in senior citizens often requires extra compassion and attention. We support elderly patients dealing with memory problems, depression, anxiety, confusion, and age-related psychological issues. With specialised evaluations and personalised care plans, we help seniors maintain dignity, clarity, and emotional well-being.

10

Relationship & Family Counselling

Conflicts within relationships can cause emotional distress for everyone involved. Our counsellors help couples and families understand communication patterns, rebuild trust, and create healthier emotional connections. Sessions provide a safe, neutral space to share feelings and learn constructive ways to resolve issues.

11

Trauma & PTSD Counselling

Traumatic experiences can deeply impact mental health. Our trauma specialists use guided therapy techniques to help patients process painful memories, reduce hypervigilance, and rebuild emotional strength. We focus on creating a safe space where healing happens without pressure.

12

Sleep Disorder Treatment

Sleep difficulties can affect your mood, focus, and daily life. Whether you’re dealing with insomnia, nightmares, or irregular sleep patterns, our psychiatrist helps identify the underlying cause and recommend a combination of therapy, medication, and lifestyle modifications for healthy sleep.

13

Anger Management Therapy

If anger is affecting your relationships or work life, we provide effective anger management training. Our counsellors help you understand triggers, manage emotional responses, and develop healthy communication techniques that reduce conflict and stress.

14

Behavioural Therapy & Psychotherapy

We offer various forms of psychotherapy tailored to your specific needs, including CBT, DBT, REBT, and supportive therapy. These therapeutic methods help you understand your emotions, break unhelpful behavioural patterns, and develop a healthier mindset.

15

Tele-Psychiatry / Online Consultations

For patients who prefer privacy or cannot visit frequently, we offer online counselling and psychiatry consultations. Through secure video calls, you can discuss concerns, receive guidance, and continue treatment from the comfort of your home.
