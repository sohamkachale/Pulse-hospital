Robotic Knee Replacement — Precision Fit, Natural Feel, Faster Recovery

Robotic assistance helps your surgeon plan and execute knee replacement with sub-millimetre accuracy and balanced ligaments. Expect smaller incisions, less pain, and a quicker return to walking. Patients visit us from Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Who Is It For?

  • Osteoarthritis with severe pain, stiffness, “bow/knock knee” deformity
  • Inflammatory arthritis with joint damage
  • Failed previous surgery needing revision

Workup & Planning

  • Orthopedic evaluation, X-rays; CT-based/imageless planning as indicated
  • Medical fitness: blood tests, ECG, chest X-ray, anaesthesia review
  • Personalized implant selection and alignment targets

Benefits of Robotic Technique

  • Accurate bone cuts & ligament balance → more natural motion
  • Smaller incision, less soft-tissue trauma & blood loss
  • Early mobilisation with ERAS pain protocols

Surgery & Recovery Path

  • Regional/general anaesthesia with multimodal pain control
  • Physiotherapy day-1 walking, stair training, home-exercise plan
  • Follow-ups for wound care, strength, and gait training

When to Visit Emergency

  • Sudden calf swelling/pain, chest pain, or breathlessness
  • High fever, wound discharge, uncontrolled pain

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced joint replacement surgeons with ICU & anaesthesia backup
  • Robotics-enabled planning, strict infection-control OT
  • Physio-led recovery, transparent costs, insurance support
