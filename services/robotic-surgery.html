Robotic Surgery — Precision, Control & Faster Recovery

Robotic assistance provides 3D vision, tremor filtration, and wristed instruments for complex procedures with smaller incisions and quicker return to routine. Multidisciplinary team with anaesthesia and ICU backup serves patients from Tathawade, Wakad, Hinjewadi, Ravet, Pimpri, and Chinchwad.

Applications

  • Urology: prostatectomy, partial nephrectomy, pyeloplasty
  • Gynecology: hysterectomy, myomectomy, endometriosis surgery
  • GI/Colorectal: rectal cancer, colectomy, foregut procedures
  • Complex hernia repairs with mesh & component separation
  • Onco-surgical pathways with organ preservation focus

Benefits

  • Smaller incisions, less pain & blood loss
  • Greater precision in narrow spaces
  • Shorter hospital stay & quicker recovery

Workup & Safety

  • Detailed pre-op assessment; imaging & anaesthesia evaluation
  • Intra-op safety checklists and ERAS protocols
  • Structured post-op pain control & early ambulation

Why Choose Pulse Hospital

  • Experienced robotic surgeons with multi-specialty support
  • Ethical counselling, clear expectations, insurance assistance
  • 24×7 Emergency & ICU for high-acuity cases
